Aircraft

An or aircraft is a piece of machinary that is capable of lifting payloads into the air. It is usually powered by fossil fuel or electric power. The main catagories of airplane are fixed-wing airplane or Helicopter. The first manned aircraft is succesfully taken off the ground on Decenmber 17, 1903. It was built by Wilbur Wright and Orville Wright.

Catagory of aircraft classification:

  • Lighter than air
    • Airships
  • Heavier than air
    • Gliders
    • Powered aircraft
      • Reciprocating engine
      • Gas turbine
      • Turbo prop
